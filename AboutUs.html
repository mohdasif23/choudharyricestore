About Us:

Since 2004, The AM Green Landscape has been a trusted partner of homeowners and businesses . We provide cost-effective residential and commercial landscaping solutions and specialize in water conservation and drought-tolerant landscapes. Our complete landscaping services include ongoing landscaping maintenance and lawn care services as well as major installation projects for landscaping, hardscapes, outdoor lighting, irrigation systems, erosion control, and drainage systems. With these complete services, our landscaping professionals will ensure that your lawn and landscaping stays looking its best. Competitively priced and always reliable, The Landscape Company has a fleet of trucks and a dedicated team of professionals, many of whom have been with the company for more than a decade, to deliver the quality and experience you desire. We serve commercial and residential customers throughout India.

Our Commitment

Our goal is to create a beautiful, sustainable outdoor space you can enjoy many years-round. We design and install landscaping and hardscaping, with a wide array of features; from patios and pergolas, to pools, spas, outdoor kitchens, and more. We also provide expert landscape maintenance through our innovative services.

Our Mission:

To enhance the beauty and value of every client’s property while exceeding their expectations every step of the way.

Our Vision

To be recognized by our clients, our team members, and experts in our industry as the best landscaping company in our market area.
